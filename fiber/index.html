01 Why Now · 为什么现在

北美市场正在打开

AI Data Centers

园区级暗光纤、园区互联、边缘接入层——非 Hyperscaler 内部 Fabric，是工程公司可参与的外围市场。
🏠
FTTX / Broadband
宽带光纤到户

🎓
Campus Fiber
园区光纤网络

⚡

Structured Cabling
结构化综合布线
商业楼宇、数据中心结构化布线——采购量稳定，承包商关系是核心资产，产品文件齐全即可进入。

从 Regional ISP、Campus Fiber、FTTX 工程商供应链切入，积累工程提交记录、客户关系和产品认可度，再逐步向高端市场延伸。

08 90-Day Launch Plan · 90天启动计划

启动不是开会，
启动是执行

Three phases, each with clear deliverables and ownership. 三个阶段，每个阶段有清晰可交付成果，不是路线图，是执行清单。

Days 0–30
Foundation & Alignment
基础对齐与架构确认
  • Confirm cooperation structure
  • Confirm monthly operating support amount
  • Assign factory contact & coordination rhythm
  • Confirm first-stage SKUs (5–8 products)
  • Create datasheet templates
  • Define showroom city
Days 30–60
Build & Recruit
资产建设与团队组建
  • Build product catalog & sample kit
  • Recruit BD & technical team members
  • Build contractor & ISP target list
  • Start LinkedIn & industry outreach
  • Begin UL / ETL preliminary review
  • Set up CRM & pipeline tracking
Days 60–90
Launch & Execute
市场启动与首批执行
  • Send first sample kits to contractors
  • Hold first customer meetings
  • Register trade shows (year calendar)
  • Push contractor onboarding
  • Receive first RFQs & prepare submittals
  • Complete first factory audit
  • Push first trial orders
09 Monthly Governance · 月度治理机制

每月一次对齐，
三方在同一频道

Transparency and accountability built into the rhythm — not improvised. 月度治理不是形式，是保持三方信息对称、资源对齐、决策透明的核心机制。

📋 Monthly Governance Meeting Agenda
  • RFQ pipeline review — 当月报价单进展
  • Customer pipeline — 客户开发进展
  • Shipment status — 发货与交付状态
  • QA issues & resolutions — 质量问题处理
  • Certification progress — 认证推进进度
  • Market execution summary — 市场执行小结
  • Decision items — 需三方确认的决策事项
📊 Monthly Report Must Include
  • 费用报告 — Expense report with breakdown
  • 客户进展 — Customer pipeline & meetings log
  • 样品进展 — Sample kit dispatch & response
  • RFQ & submittal 数量 — Volume metrics
  • 认证进展 — Certification milestone update
  • 下月重点任务 — Priority actions for next 30 days
