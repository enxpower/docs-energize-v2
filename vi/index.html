01 — Logo System
The Mark & Wordmark
The EnergizeOS identity shares the same 2×2 grid mark architecture as AGI&M ASSETS — the parent group's visual DNA. The distinction is in color: primary squares carry Slate (#1A2B3C), secondary squares carry Volt Orange (#D4840A). The diagonal A→D axis encodes forward momentum; the orange quads signal energy, high-voltage infrastructure, and operational intensity.

09 — Usage Rules
Do & Don't
These rules apply to EnergizeOS independently and as part of the AGI&M group. Any deviation requires written approval from Energize Solutions Inc.
Approved
Use the mark on approved backgrounds (white, slate, warm tint)
Maintain minimum clear space at all times
Use Volt Orange only for secondary quads, labels, and accent bars
Include the Volt bar on business cards and letterhead
Use Helvetica Neue for all brand communications
Scale the mark proportionally from master SVG files
Use the reversed logo on slate or dark backgrounds
Prohibited
Do not swap slate and volt — primary quads are always slate
Do not use volt orange as a background fill
Do not rotate, skew, or distort the mark
Do not apply gradients or drop shadows to the mark
Do not place the mark on photographic backgrounds
Do not confuse EnergizeOS assets with AGI&M assets
Do not use previous color versions of the mark
