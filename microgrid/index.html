Why it exists

Three questions every site must answer

Most EMS platforms log data. Very few help an operator or asset owner answer the questions that actually matter:

  • What happened? — Faithful, complete operational records of every dispatch, alarm, and grid event.
  • Why did it happen? — Root-cause explanation of revenue gaps, trips, and underperformance in plain language.
  • What should we do next? — Recommended actions, dispatch plans, and risk-aware decision support.

EnergizeOS Microgrid EMS is designed around these three questions — for PepsiCo-class C&I programs, EPC engineering teams, and BESS integrators.
